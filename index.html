About Us

A Personal Path to the Qur'an

Jaa Al Haq Quran Academy provides online Qur'an education for students of every age, from anywhere in the world. Our qualified Aalim-e-Deen teachers teach Noorani Qaida, Nazra, Tajweed, Hifz and Islamic Studies with full, individual attention — never in a crowded group.

We believe learning the Qur'an should feel comfortable and personal. That's why every class is one-to-one, and why brothers learn with a male teacher while sisters learn with a female teacher — so every student feels at ease from the very first lesson.

Our Courses

Choose Your Path of Learning

Noorani Qaida

The foundation of Qur'an reading — correct Arabic letters, sounds and basic rules for absolute beginners.

Nazra Quran

Fluent, correct Qur'an reading from cover to cover with ongoing correction and guidance.

Tajweed

Master the rules of proper pronunciation and recitation, the way the Qur'an was meant to be read.

Hifz-ul-Quran

Structured memorization classes with regular revision, tailored to each student's pace.

Islamic Studies

Core beliefs, seerah, fiqh essentials and Islamic manners taught in simple, clear lessons.

Daily Duas

Essential everyday duas and their meanings, for children and adults alike.
